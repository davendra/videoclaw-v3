Go Bananas style presets, and color grade suggestions.

## Seedance Omni Concept Phrasing

When using `seedance_omni.py --concept`, prepend the vibe:

- Luxury: `"Luxury [product/brand], golden hour, cinematic quality"`
- Minimal: `"Minimal [product], clean white space, soft focus"`
- Energetic: `"High energy [product/sport], dynamic movement, bold color"`
- Organic: `"Organic [product], natural setting, warm light"`
