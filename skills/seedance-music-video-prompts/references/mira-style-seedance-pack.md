# Mira-Style Seedance Music Video Pack

This reference summarizes the public Google Doc prompt pack provided by the user. Source: https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1CIf6oBPcZW0bPU8DCJW-f_x1y7me68UQQ0_ERfMlMaE/mobilebasic

## Patterns To Preserve

- Start with strong character sheets before video prompts.
- Use explicit image and audio handles: `@Image1`, `@Image2`, `@Audio1`.
- Every performance shot says the artist is actively singing or rapping the song.
- The best shots use restrained motion: small head turns, slow pushes, deliberate walking, subtle eye movement, or tiny gestures on held notes.
- Camera motion should breathe with the music rather than fight it.
- Lighting and environment should carry much of the visual energy: neon, rain, city lights, hotel interiors, diner light, golden hour, tungsten practicals.
- Negative instructions are part of the prompt, not an afterthought.

## Reusable Shot Recipes

### Character Sheet

```text
Character reference sheet of [artist description].
Layout: horizontal character sheet divided into two rows. Top row: front view, three-quarter view, profile view. Bottom row: three close-up portraits with neutral, soft smile, and profile expressions.
Neutral background, consistent lighting, sharp skin and wardrobe detail, editorial photography quality, 16:9 horizontal format.
```

### Tight Lip Sync

```text
Extreme close-up of [artist] in @Image1 performing @Audio1 to camera.
The framing holds tight on the face and upper chest. The artist actively sings or raps @Audio1, with mouth shapes matched to the vocal delivery.
Use micro-expressions, soft blinks, small eye shifts, and restrained emotion between phrases.
The camera is nearly still with a barely perceptible slow push-in.
No walking, no subtitles, no dramatic zooms, no background action.
```

### Beat-Synced Walk

```text
Wide tracking shot of [artist] in @Image1 performing @Audio1 while walking slowly toward camera through [location].
The artist sings or raps throughout the shot. Their steps land in a steady cadence with @Audio1.
The camera tracks backward smoothly at the same pace, keeping the artist centered and rhythmically connected to the song.
No exaggerated choreography, sudden camera moves, or background distractions.
```

### World-Building Performance

```text
Medium-wide shot of [artist] in @Image1 performing @Audio1 inside [cinematic location].
The artist remains the focus while the environment provides motion: rain, neon flicker, city lights, dancers, steam, curtains, or reflections.
All atmosphere moves subtly in rhythm with @Audio1.
The shot should feel like a single music-video moment, not a narrative scene with too many actions.
```

## Continuity Rules

- Keep one primary action per clip.
- Repeat the artist reference and audio handle in every video prompt.
- Name the exact wardrobe for each shot; do not let Seedance improvise outfits.
- Use one camera move per shot.
- For lip-sync shots, say "actively singing" or "actively rapping" plus "throughout the entire shot".
- End with duration and audio-native sync requirements.
