# Clone Ad Template Workflow

Analyze a reference ad into reusable structure, not copied content.

Capture:

1. hook pattern and first-second tension
2. beat order and compression strategy for 15-second variants
3. motion mode: live motion, animated stills, mixed, or unknown
4. style layers such as casting, setting, framing, lighting, pacing, and edit rhythm
5. what to keep structurally and what must change for the new intent
6. reusable variables such as product, audience, offer, proof, objection, and call to action

Clone plans should preserve the reusable mechanism while replacing product facts, claims, people, and brand-specific language.
